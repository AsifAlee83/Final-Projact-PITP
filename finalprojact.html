About Us

Founded in 2015 by Asif Ali, Khaana Peena brings the rich culinary heritage of Pakistan to your table. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, using traditional spices and cooking techniques.

Our mission: To serve authentic, hygienic, and delicious food that reminds you of home. We promise 100% halal, fresh ingredients, and generous portions.

From sizzling karahis to aromatic biryanis, every dish is crafted with love.
